Estrus period and its effect on female mouse

Like other non-primate mammals like horses, pigs and cows, mouses also has estrus cycle.
An estrus cycle is a recurring period of sexual receptivity and fertility. In easy words, female mouses want to make children in this period.
While more activity and higher temperature are expected in this period, it does not mean that estrus period is a good indicator to predict mouses' activity level and temperatures.

In the figure, the Temperature is almost the same in both periods, but the activity level is much higher in the estrus period.
Still, the activity level is not a good indicator to predict the estrus period.
